Appointment Information

📅 Regular Appointments

Schedule routine checkups, vaccinations, and non-emergency treatments during regular hours.

  • ✅ Annual wellness exams
  • ✅ Vaccination updates
  • ✅ Dental cleanings
  • ✅ Spay/neuter procedures
  • ✅ Grooming services

🚨 Emergency Services

Call immediately for urgent situations. Our emergency team is available 24/7.

  • 🚨 Severe injuries or trauma
  • 🚨 Difficulty breathing
  • 🚨 Poisoning or toxic ingestion
  • 🚨 Uncontrolled bleeding
  • 🚨 Severe pain or distress
Call Emergency: (555) 911-PETS

💡 Appointment Tips

  • 📋 Bring vaccination records
  • 📝 List current medications
  • 🍽️ Note appetite changes
  • 📱 Bring photos/videos of concerns
  • ❓ Prepare questions for the vet
